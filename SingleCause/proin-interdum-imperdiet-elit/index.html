All Project Details of Sevadham

*USAID through Govt. of India for PVOH I & II

PVOH- I & PVOH –II (Private voluntary organisation for Health)

The Ministry of health & family welfare has sanctioned rural health programme in 24 villages of Ander Maval from 1985 to 1900. The basic rural infrastructure was created in first 2 years. The 16 bedded base hospital at Vadeshwar & 7 sub centres were established. The 2nd programme of PVOH-II was implemented in 37 villages of Pait P.H. C. area in Pune district from 1992 to 1999.

With Financial assistance from Jankidevi Bajaj Gramvikas Sanstha Pune we have done plenty of family planning operations.
With the health of Mahila Mandals we have run various govt. schemes like Immunisation & care of Antenatal Mothers
*The Self Help Groups of women were established under MAVIM under I.F.A.D. assistance programme with Bank of Maharashtra. More than 20 0 self-help groups were from in Maval & Khed Taluka of Pune District. Various business activates were under taken.

Leprosy Eradication

Aided by Swiss Emmaus India And German Leprosy Relief Association (GLRA) Under this programme with counselling to family members, to patients we have cured 9000 leprosy patients in their homes with medicines & counselling. This was a great achievement.
After this, Govt. of India has also put the responsibility of Eradication of Tuberculosis under which we have cured more than 3000 patients.

RCH (Reproductive child Health)

And implemented RCH –I in 8 districts of Maharashtra through 20 field level NGO’s & RCH – II in 2 districts through FNGO’s
Maternal & New Born Health programme (Sure Start Project)
Through PCI (Projects concerned International) under Path & Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation Fund from 2007 to 2009 in Pune slum area

STI & HIV prevention programme

From 2004 to 2011 we have implemented STI & HIV prevention programme for Truckers at Nigdi Pune & Kalamboli Raigad Funded by TCI Foundation & BMGF funding. This was highly rated & selected as learning around in the Country. Since 2011 same programme is run for Migrants of Solapur District and is still going on. We have implemented

Prevention of parents to child HIV transmission (PPTCT).

In home Solapur District. It was one of the best result oriented programme. We have worked as “Nodal Agency” for HIV / AIDS for whole State of Maharashtra before the establishment MSACS (Maharashtra State Aids Control society ).
Along with these we have implemented Adolescent Reproductive & Sexual Health programme on very large scale as Family life and AIDS Education programme in 2,419 nos. of Secondary & Higher Secondary school from 21 Municipal Corporation areas of Maharashtra.
Through 69 B. Ed Colleges we have reached to 604750 students through around 7000 peer Educators & 4000 trained ‘NODEL’ Teachers. This project was implemented with the help of MSACS & UNICEF (United Nations International Children’s Emergency Fund) with greater & active implementation of State GOVT. health & Education Dept.
This programme was appreciated by NACO (National aids Control Organisation) and various international agencies and was accepted as “National Model and documented as a best practice area in international documentation.” Under these programs we have established 1592 groups of adolescent girls & 1751 groups of adolescent boys for that adolescent reproductive sexual health education. We received very good results from these groups.

We have worked as ‘lead Agency’ for “Paediatric AIDS Initiative’ programme (PAI) funded by Clinton Foundation in 13 district of Maharashtra through different implanting NGO’s.

*Shubharambh Phase-I & II -

We have implemented Health, Nutrition, Fresh vegetables and Active play programme at village Nigadi and Induri of Tahasil Maval Pune with the financial help of Mondelez through Nodal Agency Save The Childrenin year July’2014 to 2017 as Phase-I and we had stared Phase-II from Sept’ 2017 To till date through same funding agency, under this we are covering 35 schools of Maval Tahasil of Pune District of Maharashtra.
Project Goal is Improve Knowledge, Attitudes and Practise [KAP] towards nutrition and healthy lifestyle among school children age 7 to 14 years of 35 Schools.

*Watershed Development :–

A programme in area of Socio-economic Development has implemented in Khed Taluka under Draught Prone Area Programme in 5 villages and Watershed Development programme was implemented in 2 villages of Khed Taluka of Pune District with financial assistance from Germany. Another programme of Watershed Development with financial assistance from CAPART (Council of Advancement of People’s action and Rural Technologyis implemented till execution phase in Khed Taluka.

*Women Empowerment :-

We had more than one thousand Self Help Groups (SHG) with around 15,000 women members and had more than Rs. 3 Corers turnover among their own SHGs.

*Support Classes :-

We have experience of support classes in Slum area of Pune City and no of students have taken the benefit of these classes. All the Students are from low economic, social & educational background
They could not get admission in private tuitions classes. The performance of these students in school was very low. Our support classes help them in improving the knowledge of Maths, Science and English.
The majority of students were from 1st to 7th std. We have also arranged exposure visit of these students once in a quarter. Besides this we had implemented life skill sessions for adolescents.
We have vast and successful experience of Implementation, Monitoring, Evaluation of various projects and Capacity Development of NGOs of Maharashtra

*The Library at Talegaon:-

The library is established in the year 1982 for the population residing in Talegaon Station area. The Library is now recognized by Government of Maharashtra as ‘A Grade Library & according funding is received
More than 400 members are benefited at very subsidized rate of subscription.

*Asharamshala at Malegaon Budrukh ( Andar Maval):-

Sevadham found the need of school because most of the children had either never gone to school or were drop outs from schools effectively rolling back to illiteracy.
. Sevadham therefore started the residential school for tribal in 1994 with 1st std. & upgrading one class every year. 1st batch of S.S.C. appeared into 2010.
Now school has facility with residence, food, clothing including 2 sets of uniforms, toiletry, education material etc. provided completely free
We provide filtered drinking water & hot water for bath with solar heating system. Around 450 Students (girls & boys) are taking education every year.
Results of SSC examination are always above 90% . For the medical care we are providing all the required medicines & have arrangements for doctor’s visit.
We are also started M.S.F.C. [Multi Skill Foundation Courses] technical education.
In 2017 year we have started military training for 8th &9th to 10th std’s by retd. Military personnel.
School was honoured with Best Ashramshala award, amongst all the tribal schools in Thane sector of Tribal Development department of Govt. of Maharashtra, in the year 2005-2006.
For Education year 2021-22 the result for SSC Exam is 100% which makes us proud.
