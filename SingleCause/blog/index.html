*USAID through Govt. of India for PVOH I & II

PVOH- I & PVOH –II (Private voluntary organisation for Health)

The Ministry of health & family welfare has sanctioned rural health programme in 24 villages of Ander Maval from 1985 to 1900. The basic rural infrastructure was created in first 2 years. The 16 bedded base hospital at Vadeshwar & 7 sub centres were established. The 2nd programme of PVOH-II wasimplemented in 37 villages of Pait P.H. C. area in Pune district from 1992 to 1999.
