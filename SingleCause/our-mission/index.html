Our Mission

  • Our Mission

    We at Sevadham Trust aim at improving quality of life of underprivileged and underserved segments of society by providing affordable and approachable services and interventions in the field of Health & Education.

  • Our Vision

    We aim at creating society with Health and Education for all with equal opportunities to everyone for developing to be fullest one’s potential in a sustainable manner

  • Our Strength

    44 years’ experience of implementation, Monitoring and Evaluation of number of projects of various fields and categories, have a effective networking rapport and coordination with every community of project area, various Departments of State & Central Government, District, Tahashills Level Government Departments, have devoted and vast experienced staff.
    Innovative ways for Health & Education to community & using the Peers strategy & involving Peoples Participation is our great strength & we have successfully used this strategy in every programme in past.
