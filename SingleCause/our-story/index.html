Our Story- About Us

Who we are? Read our Story

Sevadham Trust is a registered Public Charitable Trust, registered under Bombay Public Trust Act 1950 (E 617) in 1978. Right from its inception in 1978 it is working for last 44 years for the welfare of under privileged & underserved community in Rural Tribal area & in urban slums.

Sevadham Trust has been active in the field of health and education for the poorest of the poor for last 44years. With the sole objective of helping the under-privileged sections of the society in their quest of a better quality of life, Sevadham has been working tirelessly in tribal and rural areas of Maharashtra, taking head-on challenges like tribal education, prevention of stigmatized diseases like – AIDS, Leprosy etc.
