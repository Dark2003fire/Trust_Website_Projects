Slum of Project Area

We have experience of support classes in Slum area of Pune City and no of students have taken the benefit of these classes. All the Students are from low economic, social & educational background. They could not get admission in private tuitions classes. The performance of these students in school was very low. Our support classes help them in improving the knowledge of Maths, Science and English. The majority of students were from 1st to 7th std. We have also arranged exposure visit of these students once in a quarter. Besides this we had implemented life skill sessions for adolescents.
