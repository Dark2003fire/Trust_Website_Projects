SEVADHAM HOSPITAL TALEGOAN

Two doctors, Dr. S. V. Gore and Dr. K. B. Wadhokar , working at Talegaon in Pune District, in early days of their career struck by the vast difference in rural & urban medical care and by extreme poverty in rural area especially the tribal population, they decided to start medical services to poor Tribal & Rural people and started small hospital at Talegaon

They were committed to provide accessible & affordable health services to all sections of society, particularly to rural poor. They aimed to be recognized by their qualitative excellence, safety & amenities. Now a days hospital is serving around 2 lakh rural population of MAVAL block, providing Multispecialty services under one roof with 24 X 7 accessibility at affordable cost. Our Hospital is having ISO certification.

Sevadham Hospital is one of the projects of Sevadham Trust registered under Public Charitable Trust. Sevadham Trust is recognized by the Govt. of India as mother agency for HIV & AIDS prevention programmes . The trust works in rural, tribal & hilly areas through number of static, mobile clinics & outreach activities to give doorstep service. The Trust also provides consulting & hospital facilities in remote areas.

Today Sevadham Hospital is ISO 9001:2015 recognized Institute. It is trying to cater health needs of urban population of Talegaon & a vast number of rural populations of Maval Taluka spread in more than 165 villages & adjoining field areas.
