Save the children Induri clinic

A small amount can change the life of a child; through education, nutrition, and basic protection, children can lead normal livesour contribution has the power to uplift children in dire situations. We’re working towards a nation where its children live a secure life, full of opportunities for growth and development.

Sevadham Trust located in Maharashtra is actively working on projects related to rural healthcare. The organization strives day and night to take care of the well being of vulnerable children and women and facilitate them with primary healthcare services that include basic checks ups, treatment, and nutritional supplements. Hospitals are also set up to help the differently-abled /poor people in times of need. Educational, counseling, vocational training, and awareness programs are also conducted to help these poor/ deprived people get aware of the life-threatening diseases such as HIV/AIDS and learn how to maintain a clean, hygienic lifestyle.

Slum of Project Area

We have experience of support classes in Slum area of Pune City and no of students have taken the benefit of these classes. All the Students are from low economic, social & educational background. They could not get admission in private tuitions classes.
The performance of these students in school was very low. Our support classes help them in improving the knowledge of Maths, Science and English. The majority of students were from 1st to 7th std. We have also arranged exposure visit of these students once in a quarter. Besides this we had implemented life skill sessions for adolescents.
